EXPECTAIONS vs REALITY of college

College life have a great influence on our life. It provides us the opportunity to explore new aspects of life and collaborate with others and develop our social and technical skills.

College marks beginning of new phase of our life.Everyone of us have expectations regarding our college.Let us explore how much of our generalisation were actually true.

Campus size

Expecation

Most of us wish that campus of the college should be huge with all sorts of facilities like sportfield,auditorium,night clubs within its boundaries.

Reality

Campus size is not large and not even permanent.

Mess

Expectation

We expected that every kind of meal with quality of no match would be available to us.

Reality

Food is actually of decent quality with some variety available to us.

Sports

Expectation

we expected a seperate building for indoor games ,large ground for outdoor games and a gym with all those equipment provided to a professional.

Reality

THere is no seperate building for games or a gym .

Power supply

Expectation

We expected a 24/7 power supply and super powerful diesel generator that works right after any power cut.

Reality

Power cut are actually quiet frequent and generator works at fixed times.

Internet connection

Expectation

High speed internet (probably 6G)with superb connectivity.

Reality

Speed is actually good but connectivity is not very good(network problem!)

Room size

Expectation

Most of us expected the room size to be small and acoomodate 2 or 3 people as we heard it from our elder sibling living in hostels.

Reality

I was excieted to live in a flat.Rooms are actually large with lobby and balcony.8 students live in one flat!

Course

Expectation

We expected to study majorly programming only and assignment would be less.

Reality

In reality we study many subjects like enginerring physics mathematics other than programming language.We get lot of assignment and there is so much to study.
